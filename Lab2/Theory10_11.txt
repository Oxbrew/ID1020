10) The list below is partially ordered by insertion sort up until 5.
How many comparisons and swaps are needed to sort the 5?
[1 3 4 8 9 5 2]

ANSWER:
3 comparisons
2 Swaps

[1 3 4 8 5 9 2] SWAPS 5 and 9 + Compare

[1 3 4 5 8 9 2] SWAPS 5 and 8 + Compare

[1 3 4 5 8 9 2] COMPARES 5 and 4
